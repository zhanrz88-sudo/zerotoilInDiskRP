# ZeroToil

ZeroToil is a human-in-the-loop AI initiative to reduce operational toil and improve mitigation reliability by automating end-to-end **Xstore ICM incident** Troubleshooting Guides (TSGs).

## What it does

- Automates repeatable TSG steps end-to-end to **shorten time-to-mitigation**.
- Uses LLMs to **accelerate authoring and maintenance** of the automation code.
- Keeps engineers **in control** of production-impacting actions (review/approve/execute).

## Scope

- Focus: AI-assisted automation of Xstore ICM incident mitigation TSGs.
- Outcome: consistent, repeatable mitigations with reduced manual effort.

## Adoption principles

- Staged rollout with explicit safety guardrails.
- Risk-conscious design to be safe, reliable, and scalable for engineering leadership, automation architects, and DevOps teams.
